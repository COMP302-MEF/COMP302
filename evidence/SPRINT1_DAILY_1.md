## Sprint 1 Notes

Sprint 1 focused on the technical and functional foundation of the project. The team prepared the database connection, basic models, authentication endpoints, initial frontend pages, and basic course/activity listing.

The AI tutoring flow, objective-based scoring, manual grading, reset behavior, and final demo evidence were planned for Sprint 2 because they depend on the Sprint 1 foundation.

## Sprint 1 Output

At the end of Sprint 1, the project had:

- PostgreSQL connection.
- Basic backend structure.
- User, Course, Enrollment, and Activity models.
- Login and register flow.
- Instructor and student role field.
- Course listing endpoint.
- Activity listing endpoint.
- Initial instructor dashboard.
- Initial student panel.
