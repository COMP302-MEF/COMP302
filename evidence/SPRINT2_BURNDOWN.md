## Burndown Summary

Sprint 2 started with 75 story points of planned work. The sprint included the main demo-critical functionality: token-based authorization, instructor activity management, student ACTIVE activity access, AI tutoring, objective-based scoring, score logs, manual grading, reset behavior, and final evidence preparation.

The actual burndown was slower than ideal in the first half because server-side authorization and frontend/backend integration required extra testing. The team recovered in the second half after the AI chat, scoring, logs, manual grade, and reset features were completed.
